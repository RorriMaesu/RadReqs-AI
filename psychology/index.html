Expert Guide: Rules of Defensible Charting

The Golden Rule: If it wasn't documented, it wasn't done. Your electronic health record (EHR) is a legal document. It must be strictly objective, factual, and free of personal bias.

Poor (Subjective)

"The patient was acting crazy and got very angry about their medication. They rudely refused to take it."

  • "Crazy" and "Angry" are emotional assumptions.
  • "Rudely" is a personal judgment.

Excellent (Objective)

"Patient paced the room, speaking in a loud volume. Patient stated, 'I am not taking that poison!' and swatted the medication cup away."

  • Documents specific behaviors (pacing, swatting).
  • Uses direct quotes for exact context.
